Mia Catalano is a bicoastal musical theatre and on–camera performer. Her love for the stage began in 2015, and since then, she has been in over 20 plays and musicals.

A lifelong creative, she grewup taking singing lessons and acting classes, writing screenplays and making films, and producing plays she'd written. Today, her creative spark fuels her passion for theatre. When she is not on stage, she loves to stay busy with one of her many hobbies, like boxing, piano, gaming, baking, or petting strangers' dogs.

Mia is currently studying psychology at California State University, Northridge, where she recently played Titania in A Midsummer Night's Dream. Beyond college, her credits include Fantine in Les Miserables, Pennywise in Urinetown, and Hope in Almost, Maine.
